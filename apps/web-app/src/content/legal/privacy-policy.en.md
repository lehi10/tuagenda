# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 23, 2026

## 1. Introduction

TuAgenda ("we", "our", or "the platform") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and what your rights are, in compliance with **Peruvian Law No. 29733 on Personal Data Protection** and its regulations.

## 2. Data Controller

**TuAgenda** — 100% remote company, operating from Lima, Peru.
Contact email: soporte@tuagenda.pe

TuAgenda acts as the **data controller** for the personal data of users registered on the platform. With respect to end customer data managed by businesses, TuAgenda acts as a **data processor** on behalf of the corresponding business.

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Data you provide to us

**Registered users (businesses and customers with accounts):**

- First and last name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Date of birth (optional)
- Profile photo (optional)

**Businesses:**

- Business name, description, and logo
- Address, city, and country
- Billing and subscription information

**End customers (with or without an account):**

- First and last name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Appointment history and contracted services
- Notes related to appointments (e.g., service instructions)

> **Important:** We do not store banking data or credit/debit card information. Online payments are processed directly by the enabled payment gateways under their own security policies.

### 3.2 Automatically collected data

- IP address and approximate geolocation data
- Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
- Pages visited, features used, and time spent
- Session data generated by Firebase Authentication

### 3.3 Third-party data

- **Google Analytics 4:** browsing behavior and usage metrics
- **Tawk.to:** live support conversations
- **Brevo:** interactions with emails sent by the platform

## 4. Purpose of Processing

We use your data for the following purposes:

- **Providing the Service:** creating and managing your account, processing bookings and payments
- **Communications:** sending appointment confirmations, service notifications, and, with your consent, marketing communications via Brevo
- **Support:** handling your inquiries and requests via Tawk.to and email
- **Security:** detecting and preventing fraud, unauthorized access, and other malicious activities
- **Service improvement:** analyzing platform usage with Google Analytics 4 to improve features and experience
- **Legal compliance:** fulfilling applicable legal and regulatory obligations

## 5. Business Access to Customer Data

Businesses using TuAgenda have access to the data of their customers registered on the platform, including name, email address, phone number, appointment history, and associated notes. This access is necessary for the delivery of the appointment management service.

Businesses do **not have access** to passwords or authentication credentials. Passwords are managed in encrypted form by **Firebase Authentication** (Google LLC), and neither TuAgenda nor the businesses can access them at any time.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following tracking technologies:

- **Firebase Authentication:** technical session cookies, required for the Service to function. They cannot be disabled without affecting access to your account.
- **Google Analytics 4:** behavioral analytics cookies. They collect usage data in an aggregated and pseudonymous form to improve the platform.
- **Tawk.to:** live chat cookies for user support.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling technical cookies may prevent access to your account.

## 7. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored and processed on servers located in the **United States** (Amazon Web Services and Google Cloud Platform). This international transfer is carried out under contractual guarantees that ensure a level of protection equivalent to that required by **Law No. 29733** and its regulations.

Third-party services used (Google, Tawk.to, Brevo) may also process data in their own international infrastructure, subject to their respective privacy policies.

## 8. Data Retention

| Data type                        | Retention period                      |
| -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Active user account              | While the account is active           |
| Business data after cancellation | 3 months from cancellation            |
| Inactive guest accounts          | 12 months from last activity          |
| Data required by law             | As required by applicable regulations |

After these periods, data will be permanently deleted or anonymized.

## 9. Your Rights

In accordance with **Law No. 29733**, you have the following rights over your personal data:

- **Access:** request information about the data we hold about you
- **Rectification:** correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Cancellation:** request the deletion of your data and account
- **Objection:** object to the processing of your data for specific purposes, such as marketing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **soporte@tuagenda.pe**. We will respond to your request within a maximum of **1 month** from receipt.

> If you request deletion of your account, your data will be deleted within a maximum of 30 days. Appointment history associated with a business may be retained in anonymized form for statistical purposes.

## 10. Data Security

We implement the following technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication managed by Firebase Authentication
- Role-based access control within the platform
- Infrastructure on AWS and GCP with redundancy and backups
- Restricted access to sensitive data by TuAgenda staff

However, no system is 100% infallible. In the event of a security breach affecting your data, we will notify you as soon as possible in accordance with applicable regulations.

## 11. Minors

TuAgenda does not intentionally collect personal data from individuals under 18 years of age. If we detect that data from a minor has been registered without adult supervision, we will proceed to delete it. If you become aware of such a situation, please contact us at soporte@tuagenda.pe.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of material changes via email or through the platform. Continued use of the Service after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
